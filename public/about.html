About Mixr

Mixr is a web application made for cocktail lovers who want to explore different types of cocktails or even find new ones.

How to Use

Home Page- Search for different cocktail names and ingredients. You can filter different ingredients to view cocktails based on that.


Cocktails Page - View your results on specific ingredients or searched cocktails. View a picture of the cocktail and a description.
